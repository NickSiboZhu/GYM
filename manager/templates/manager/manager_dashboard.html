Welcome to the GYM Manager System

Management Operation Guide

In our GYM Manager System, you can perform various management tasks through the following interfaces:

  • Coaches: Add, edit, and delete coaches in the system. View coach information such as name, ID, sex, phone number, and avatar.
  • Supplements Add,edit, and delete supplements in the system. View supplement information such as name. price, stock, and image.
  • Courses: Add, edit, and delete courses. View course information such as name, time, coach, and block.
  • Equipment: Add, edit, and delete gym equipment. View equipment information such as name and associated block.
  • Membership cardsView all the member information such as name, account, customer ID, start date and end date.
  • Personal InformationView all the customer information such as ID, name, sex, phone number, account and balance.
  • Data_visual: View the number of coaches, supplements, equipments and customers.

Navigate to the respective interface using the sidebar menu to perform the desired management tasks.
